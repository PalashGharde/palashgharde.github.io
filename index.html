// Who I Am
ABOUT ME

Hey there, I'm Palash! I'm a Technical Game Designer focused on building gameplay systems, rapid prototypes, and interactive level experiences in Unreal Engine and Unity. My work combines design thinking with a strong software engineering foundation, and I have built multiple game projects with a focus on modular gameplay systems, AI behavior, interaction systems, procedural systems, and player-centered mechanics.

I started my game development journey while working full-time as a Software Developer, where I realized I wanted to combine my love for games with my interest in coding. I began learning Unity and Blender in my free time, then later picked up Unreal Engine during my master's degree and became drawn to Blueprint scripting for rapid prototyping. Since then, I have built games in Unreal Engine and Unity, focusing on gameplay mechanics, interactive systems, and NPC AI using Behavior Trees, Blackboards, and AI Perception. My software development background helps me build systems that are logical, robust, and scalable.

I'm now looking to join the game industry full-time as a Technical Game Designer, where I can contribute to meaningful gameplay experiences as part of a collaborative team. Whether it's prototyping a new mechanic, scripting interactive systems, building NPC behavior, improving level flow, or supporting designers with technical implementation, I'm excited to help bring creative ideas to life.

// Blueprint Implementation
Pickup, Interact & Elemental Systems — Blueprint Code
All core mechanics are implemented in Blueprint. Three interconnected systems handle everything from object interaction to elemental chain reactions.
Pickup & Place — BP_ThirdPersonCharacter
  • Tap F — pick up object (Started action)
  • Hold F — show placement preview mesh
  • Release F — place if location valid
  • Green material = valid surface / Red = invalid
  • BPI_PickableObjects interface on all pickable actors
  • BPC_CheckPlacingSurface component on BP_IceCube & BP_WoodStack
Interact — BP_RotatingLever
  • E key — enter / exit lever interaction mode
  • W / S — rotate lever while in interaction mode
  • Sphere trace in BP_ThirdPersonCharacter detects interactables in front of player
  • Same E key used for door interaction and ladder mount
Elemental Chain — Interface-Driven
  • BP_Fireball hits WoodStack → triggers BPI_BurnableObjects
  • Burning wood propagates heat → BPI_HeatableObjects on BP_WaterContainer
  • BP_WaterContainer detects BP_IceCube above and melt it → fills container with water -> after ice completely melts, the water starts boiling.
  • Boiling water generates steam → pushes lift's counterweight up -> lift comes down
  • Water evaporation timer → lift's counterweight descends automatically after water evaporates completely -> lift goes up

Step-by-Step Gameplay Walkthrough
F  Pick / Place E  Interact WASD  Move W/S  Lever / Ladder RMB  Aim LMB  Shoot Fireball
1
Place Wood Stack
Walk near Wood Stack → Tap F to pick up. Hold F to see preview, walk to Yellow Plate, Release F to place.
2
Place Ice Cube in Container
Pick up Ice Cube → go to the top floor → place ice inside the green water container using Hold & Release F.
3
Move Container with Lever
Approach first lever → E to enter lever mode → W/S to rotate → position the green container below the lift's counterweight (orange box) → E to exit.
4
Move Log Plate with Second Lever
Use the second lever the same way — rotate to slide the log plate directly below the green container so the fire can heat it.
5
Shoot Fireball — Start the Chain
Right click to aim → Left click to shoot fireball at the Wood Stack. Fire burns wood → heats container → melts ice → water boils → generates steam (~45 sec chain).
6
Ride the Steam Lift
While wood burns and ice melts, go to the lift entrance. Wait for the counterweight to rise and the lift to come down. Enter and wait — lift rises automatically once steam is fully active, then descends when water evaporates.
7
Open the Door & Climb Ladder
Find the door blocking the stairs on the right → E to open. Interact with ladder → W to climb up, S to climb down to reach the next stage.
8
Exit — Cutscene Trigger PROTOTYPE
Used a temporary teleport box as a lift to reach the exit as lift system not implemeted yet.
